A human–AI collaboration framework enhances digital pathology

Combining human expertise with AI can significantly enhance the quality, speed, and confidence of surgical pathologists’ assessments, ultimately contributing to better clinical workflow and patient care.

Project Highlights

  • Human-AI collaboration framework.
  • Active learning with human-in-the-loop.
  • Rapidly create a dataset in real-time.
  • Faster and Better diagnosis.

Takeaway message

Nuclei.io enables pathologists to rapidly create diverse datasets and models for multiple clinically critical applications. To validate the effectiveness of this framework, we perform two cross-over user studies using a human–AI collaborative approach. In both studies, the use of AI yielded considerable improvements in sensitivity, certainty, and efficiency in pathological diagnosis. Our findings validate the proposed human-in-the-loop framework, and suggest that human–AI collaboration holds the potential to revolutionize digital pathology, ultimately leading to improved quality, efficiency, and cost.
